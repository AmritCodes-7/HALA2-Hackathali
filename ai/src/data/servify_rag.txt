Company Name: Servify

About Servify:
Servify is a SaaS (Software as a Service) company founded on February 21, 2026, that provides instant home services to users in Nepal. The platform connects users with nearby verified professional service providers quickly and efficiently. Servify is built to solve the problem of finding trusted, skilled professionals for home repair and maintenance tasks.

Services Offered:
- Electrician services (wiring, short circuit repair, appliance installation, meter issues)
- Plumber services (pipe repair, leakage fix, tap installation, drainage issues)
- Carpenter services (furniture repair, door/window fixing, custom woodwork)
- Professional consultation services (get advice before booking a full service)

How It Works:
1. User opens the Servify app or website
2. User searches for the service they need (e.g. "plumber" or "electrician")
3. Servify detects the user's location automatically
4. The platform displays the nearest available and verified professionals
5. User selects a professional and books instantly
6. The professional arrives at the user's location and completes the job
7. User rates the professional after the service

Key Features:
- Location-based service discovery (shows nearest professionals in real time)
- Instant home service booking (no waiting, no phone calls)
- Certificate verification system (all professionals are verified by AI)
- Professional consultation available before booking
- Fast support for users in need of home services
- Real-time availability (see who is online and ready)
- Rating and review system for professionals

Pricing:
- Consultation: Free for the first 15 minutes
- Electrician visit: Starting from NPR 500
- Plumber visit: Starting from NPR 400
- Carpenter visit: Starting from NPR 600
- Final pricing depends on the complexity of the job
- No hidden charges — professionals provide a quote before starting work

Booking Policy:
- Users can book a service anytime during support hours
- Bookings can be cancelled up to 30 minutes before the scheduled visit
- Cancellations after 30 minutes may incur a small fee of NPR 100
- Rescheduling is free if done 1 hour in advance

Refund Policy:
- Full refund if no professional is found within 30 minutes
- Partial refund if service is cancelled mid-way due to professional's fault
- No refund for completed services unless there is a verified complaint

Service Areas:
- Currently serving Kathmandu Valley (Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur)
- Expanding to Pokhara and Chitwan by mid-2026

Target Users:
- Homeowners and residents who need quick home repair or maintenance services
- Renters who need minor fixes without involving landlords
- People looking to consult with professionals for home-related issues
- Offices and small businesses needing maintenance support

Business Model:
Servify operates as a SaaS platform, connecting service seekers with skilled professionals. Servify earns a small commission per completed booking. Professionals pay a monthly subscription to be listed on the platform.

Professional Verification:
- All professionals must submit their skill certificates
- Certificates are verified using Servify's AI-powered certificate validation system
- Professionals are rated by users after each job
- Professionals with ratings below 3.0 are suspended pending review
- Background checks are conducted for all new professionals

FAQ:
Q: How do I book a service?
A: Open the app, search for the service you need, select a nearby professional, and confirm the booking. It takes less than 2 minutes.

Q: Are the professionals trustworthy?
A: Yes. All professionals on Servify are verified through our AI certificate validation system and background checks.

Q: What if the professional doesn't show up?
A: You will receive a full refund and we will assign a new professional immediately.

Q: Can I talk to a professional before booking?
A: Yes. Use the free consultation feature to chat or call a professional before committing to a booking.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Cash on delivery, eSewa, Khalti, and bank transfer.

Q: How do I cancel a booking?
A: Go to "My Bookings" in the app, select the booking, and tap "Cancel". Cancellations are free if done 30 minutes before the visit.

Q: What areas does Servify serve?
A: Currently Kathmandu Valley. Expanding to Pokhara and Chitwan by mid-2026.

Q: How long does it take for a professional to arrive?
A: Usually within 30-60 minutes depending on your location and availability.
