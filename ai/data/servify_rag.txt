Company Name: Servify

About Servify:
Servify is a SaaS (Software as a Service) company that provides instant home services to users. The platform connects users with nearby professional service providers quickly and efficiently.

Services Offered:
- Electrician services
- Plumber services
- Professional consultation services

How It Works:
- Users can search for the service they need
- Servify displays the nearest available service providers based on the user's location
- Users can get fast support and instant assistance

Key Features:
- Location-based service discovery (shows nearest professionals)
- Instant home service booking
- Professional consultation available
- Fast support for users in need of home services

Target Users:
- Homeowners and residents who need quick home repair or maintenance services
- People looking to consult with professionals for home-related issues

Founding Date: February 21, 2026

Business Model:
Servify operates as a SaaS platform, connecting service seekers with skilled professionals like electricians and plumbers in their vicinity.
